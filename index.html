Who we are?

We are a dedicated scout group committed to building character, leadership, and a spirit of service in young individuals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower youth through adventure, education, and community service, shaping them into responsible and confident citizens.

Our vision

Our vision is to create a generation of compassionate, skilled, and resilient leaders who contribute positively to their communities and the world.

Rover stages

Squire Stage

Initial 3-month probation period before becoming a full Rover.

One year of advanced training to develop leadership and skills.
